Message Decoded!

“Pleasant words are as an honeycomb, sweet to the soul, and health to the bones.” — Proverbs 16:24

Solomon tells us that kind, pleasant words are like honey — sweet and healing. What comes out of our mouths reveals what is in our hearts. Let’s use our tongues to speak words that bless others!
